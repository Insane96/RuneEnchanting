## Summary

Rune Enchanting replaces vanilla's enchanting table with a simpler, exploration-driven system: find Runes in the world and socket them into your gear using an Anvil.

## The mod

Rune Enchanting creates a new system to empower your items:

*   Find runes in chests or by breaking mob spawners

    ![Find runes in chests](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/FB1dDbCy/images/3f874574bc37454295c8214163d6ff049ec7c0ec.png)

*   Apply them to items with an Anvil (runes are basically re-balanced versions of vanilla enchantments and have only one level, but there are also many new ones)

    ![Apply them via Anvils](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/FB1dDbCy/images/efca7478e697017a2704147d680b5e055e6d36f2.png)

*   Each material has its own number of slots, more for lower tier items (e.g. Wood has more slots compared to Netherite)

    ![Sockets](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/FB1dDbCy/images/b48a547fc46ef56dfa0b4b7a8c19ed29d72dc195.png)

*   You can extract runes as you wish with Grindstone

*   Curses are a special type of rune that don't use up a slot and increase the max slots by 1. These can't be extracted and the curses are hidden. Many curses have been added.

    ![Curses](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/FB1dDbCy/images/225ca0dd2bfa9d7da73891be9b0af0fc5cd0906f.png)
